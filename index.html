Its Women's Equality Day.

"I'd be a fearless leader I'd be an alpha type When everyone believes ya What's that like?"
Being a woman comes with so many situations where she asks this question to this society and to herself. She dreams of a world where she can lead alongside others. She desire equal respect, opportunities, the right to chase their dreams and passions, the right to live safely. This has always been a very sensitive yet essential topic, Women's equality. While there's nothing that should be stopping the genders from being treated equally, why is it that we still have to live with those principles and beliefs and a mindset where women can not grow and bloom with men. This needs reform, it is reforming and the reform must go on!
